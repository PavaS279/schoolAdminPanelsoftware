Welcome to
National Public School
Rajajinagar

NPS started its journey in the year 1959 under the pioneering effort and careful nurturing by our Founder Chairman Dr. K P Gopalkrishna and Senior Principal Dr. Santhamma Gopalkrishna. NPS Rajajinagar has been known for its academic excellence, discipline and ethical practices.
Today NPS has more than 1800 students on its roll and has the honour of being granted Autonomous status by the CBSE, Govt. of India. This is an honour given only to two schools in the country of which NPS Rajajinagar is one. Every year batches of students leave its portals as assured confident young citizens, ready to take on the challenges of life and equipped with an enduring set of values.
